Why Define User Persona?

Its about the definition of a user representing a group of individuals of the target product
The user persona represent a significant portion of people that allow us focus our solutions in real individuals instead of trying to cover a target too generic‚Äù.

This technique, applied in processes focused on users, converts the data collected in the target's research representative archetypes that will help us create the right paths for your experience to be optimal. The design of people is based on the objectives, behavior, and concerns of users that concern our product or service. It is a perfect methodology to face users and be able to understand them, thus focusing the project on the satisfaction of their concrete needs.
